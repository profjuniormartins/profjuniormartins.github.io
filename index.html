Raimundo Martins de Araújo-Júnior, Esp.

Assistent Professor
Federal University of West Pará (UFOPA)

I am an Assistent Professor at Federal University of West Pará (UFOPA) in the course bachelor Information Systems since January/2018. I hold a Specialization (2017) in Computer networks from Federal University Technological of Paraná, and Bachelor degree (2015) in Information Systems at Metropolitan College of Marabá.


Between 2016-2017 I was substitute teacher in the Federal University of Southern and Southeastern Pará.

Currently, I'm doing my masters degree in Computer Science from the Federal University of Pernambuco (UFPE). I'm working on research about blockchain-based health information systems. However, I also work with other areas such as Software-Defined-Networking (SDN), Computer Information Security and Computer in Education.

Projects

LAPIS

The project aims to research the best development practices for the Android platform and perform analysis, development, testing and validation of applications so that it can meet the needs and peculiarities of the city of Oriximiná and also the region, enabling, through applications, provide solutions to such needs. This will also contribute to the technical and professional growth of undergraduate students at UFOPA who will produce software products and scientific articles seeking publications, in addition to creating a favorable environment for high school students to enter a technological environment.

Members: Raimundo Martins de Araújo Júnior - Coordinator.


Situation: In progress; Nature: Research.

Introduction to Cisco Networking (Routing and Switching) [Extension]

The main objective of this project is to facilitate knowledge for high school students in the municipality of Oriximiná, western Pará. They will learn the basics of communication networks, learning from building a topology to performing basic and advanced routing.

Members: Raimundo Martins de Araújo Júnior - Coordinator / Gilnei Samuel - Member / Sueli Silva - Member.
